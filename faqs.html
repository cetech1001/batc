Frequently Asked Questions

Great minds think alike! Sometimes, they even have the same questions.

  • What is Bolanle Addiction Treatment Center?

    We’re a top-rated humanitarian organization
    But our mission goes well beyond our great city: We help people around the world break free from addiction. Through a proven approach, local staff in each city and 67 community centers strategically located in various communities, work to provide the people in these communities a safe space where they can talk about the traumatic ordeals which led to their addiction and help them battle these addictions.

    Over the past years, we’ve developed a high-impact methodology that gives people with addiction the tools they need to create brighter futures for themselves and their communities. With your support, they receive access to health care, educational resources and life-changing programs in safe, clean spaces.

  • Who do you work for?

    Our communities. Lots of rural communities in the country have a high ratio of people battling addiction.

  • How did you get started (and when)?

    We've been around a while. Back in 2019, our organization was originally dedicated to healing disabled children and providing outreach to widows and orphans. Today, we serve more than a million people battling addiction.

  • What’s your goal?

    To help cure addiction. And we believe the best way to do this is to heal people at a community level.
    Most people battling addiction have very little control over their lives and futures, so we’re working to change that. We believe we can make a difference in the world and addiction is a solvable issue. We also believe once you break the cycle of addiction for one community, you’ll impact generations to come.
    Our goal is every child graduates from our program healthy, educated, employed and empowered to break the cycle of poverty.

  • Are you a religious and/or political organization?

    No, our supporters, volunteers, staff, children and their families span all beliefs and backgrounds. And we all share the same goal: breaking the cycle of poverty.

  • Who works at your organization?

    We have about 200 amazing people here in Kansas City, Missouri, and around 1,200 more fantastic in-country team members who support our agencies and community centers around the world.

  • How can I work at your organization?

    We’re looking for the best, brightest and most passionate people on the planet. You can check our career opportunities to see openings.
